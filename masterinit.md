BUSINESS & TECHNICAL PROPOSAL: LINE-INTEGRATED INTERACTIVE DESIGNER PLATFORMEXECUTIVE SUMMARYThis proposal outlines the strategic development of an Interactive Jewelry Customizer (2D Canvas Assembly Engine) integrated directly within the LINE Ecosystem. By combining a seamless LINE-First onboarding flow with an intuitive customizer, this system transforms traditional e-commerce into an automated, highly personalized, and high-margin sales engine.Developed by DevDD, this platform is designed as a production-grade, enterprise-ready solution that bridges the gap between interactive front-end visual experiences and secure cloud-based payment backend systems.1. Onboarding (LINE-First): The customer scans a QR code or clicks a link. They are instantly redirected to the brand's LINE Official Account (LINE OA). Before entering the designer, the user is automatically registered, and their profile is securely bound to our database.Launch (LINE LIFF): Within the chat interface, a LINE LIFF (LINE Front-end Framework) window opens as an elegant, fast-loading overlay.Interactive Designing: The customer customizes their bracelet using the intuitive canvas, selecting stones, sizes, spacers, and charms. The price recalculates instantly based on materials used.Seamless checkout: Upon design completion, the system generates a dynamic Thai PromptPay QR code or routes the user directly to a payment gateway within the same LIFF window.Status Notifications: After payment, LINE OA automatically sends transaction receipts and subsequent progress updates (e.g., "Designing", "Assembling", "Shipped").2. MONETIZATION ARCHITECTURE (HOW THIS MAKES MONEY)A. Real-Time Visual Upselling (Average Order Value Lift)Rather than buying a fixed-price product, customers design their own experiences.Dynamic Premium Upgrades: As users swap standard beads for premium raw gemstones or sterling silver spacers, the system updates the price in real-time. This interactive experience naturally drives higher Average Order Value (AOV), as customers justify the incremental costs of their custom creations.Add-On Recommendation Engine: Before checking out, the system suggests context-aware add-ons (e.g., matching custom rings, gift wrapping, or premium velvet boxes) directly within the canvas, leading to an estimated 15-25% increase in total bill size.B. Cart Abandonment Automatic RecoveryTargeted Push Recovery: If a customer designs a beautiful jewelry piece but exits before checking out, our database retains the exact designed configuration.Personalized Re-engagement: The system triggers an automated LINE message 2 hours later, displaying an image of their exact customized design with a tailored offer (e.g., "Your custom creation is waiting. Complete checkout now to get free shipping"). This utilizes LINE Push Messages, which have a drastically higher open rate (over 80%) compared to email marketing.C. White-Label SaaS Platform ModelMulti-Tenant Capability: The core engine is designed as a scalable SaaS. DevDD can license this interactive designer to other boutique jewelry, fashion, or custom apparel brands as a white-label service.Subscription & Licensing Revenue: Charges can be structured as a monthly base subscription fee + a minor percentage fee per transaction processed through the canvas engine.3. STRATEGIC BUSINESS IMPACT (HOW THIS IMPROVES BUSINESS)A. Frictionless Conversion (No App Install Required)By embedding the customizer directly inside LINE LIFF, we bypass the biggest drop-off point in mobile commerce: app installation. Users do not need to download a standalone iOS/Android application, creating a seamless, one-tap entry from marketing campaigns straight into the design funnel.B. Automated Production & Inventory ManagementInstant Bill of Materials (BOM) Generation: When a purchase is completed, the system automatically translates the custom visual design into a structured work order (e.g., Gemstone A: 10 units, spacer B: 2 units, length: 17cm).Zero Production Lag: This digital blueprint is instantly routed to the fulfillment team's assembly screen or printing queue, reducing human error in custom assembly to 0% and cutting processing times by 40%.Automatic Inventory Holds: The engine references live inventory. If a specific gemstone is out of stock, it is dynamically disabled in the customer-facing editor to avoid over-selling issues.C. Zero-Cost Re-Marketing ChannelBecause every customer is locked into the LINE OA ecosystem before they begin designing, the brand builds an extremely high-value, direct-to-consumer communication channel. Future promotions, new collections, or seasonal discounts can be broadcasted directly to warm audiences with zero ad spend.4. Client Side & Visual EngineLINE LIFF API: Handles seamless authentication, user profile binding, and in-app checkout flows within the LINE client.High-Performance 2D/3D Canvas Engine: Uses custom WebGL or canvas-rendering layers to swap gemstone assets, textures, and charms smoothly, ensuring ultra-fast load times even under mobile data.State Management: Captures the precise arrangement coordinates of jewelry assets to generate exact output blueprints for production.B. Backend Pricing & Fulfillment EngineDynamic Price Matrix: Evaluates individual item SKU costs, dynamically calculating the final price and generating dynamic payment payloads.BOM Processor: Automatically converts visual state JSONs into clean print-ready orders and inventory deduction calls.Payment Webhook System: Integrates directly with standard dynamic PromptPay APIs, verifying payments in milliseconds and updating order statuses dynamically.C. Infrastructure & SecurityHigh-Availability Infrastructure (Vishnu): Running on isolated, high-performance on-premise clusters (via Proxmox) with hybrid failover configurations (AWS CDN) to handle massive spikes in web traffic during peak marketing hours.Zero-Trust Token Security: Utilizing Infisical for secure API key injection and Tailscale secure overlays, protecting client financial data and preventing unauthorized manipulation of gemstone pricing on the client side.CONCLUSIONBy implementing the LINE-Integrated Interactive Designer, businesses can leverage the familiarity of Thailand's most used messaging app while deploying an advanced, high-yielding visual customizer. This solution is not just an e-commerce catalog; it is an optimized system designed to drive customer engagement, secure higher margins, and automate back-office assembly operations.
